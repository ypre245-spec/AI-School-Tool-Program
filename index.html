Our Mission

"Bagong Pilipinas, Bagong Edukasyon, Bagong Teknolohiya."

The AI School Tool Program aims to bridge the gap between traditional pedagogy and modern technology. We believe that Artificial Intelligence should not replace teachers, but rather serve as a powerful assistant that amplifies their capacity to inspire and educate.

By providing accessible, free, and curriculum-aligned AI tools, we are committed to supporting the Department of Education's vision of producing resilient and competent learners.
